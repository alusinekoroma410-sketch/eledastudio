Welcome to the Sierra Leone Football Association

Growing football across Sierra Leone with passion and integrity

The Sierra Leone Football Association (SLFA) governs, promotes and develops football at all levels across the country — from grassroots programs to national teams. We are committed to transparency, talent development, and delivering memorable experiences for fans and players alike.

About Us

History and Purpose

Founded to organize and chart the growth of football in Sierra Leone, the SLFA oversees domestic competitions, amateur and professional leagues, national teams, and youth development. We operate with transparency, fostering partnerships with clubs, communities and international federations to deliver sustainable progress.

What we do

  • Organize national competitions and cup tournaments.
  • Develop coaching and refereeing standards.
  • Support grassroots academies and school programs.
  • Manage national teams and international representation.

Values

Integrity, inclusion, excellence and community. We are committed to providing equal opportunities and creating a safe, competitive environment for all participants.
